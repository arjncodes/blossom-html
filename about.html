About Us

Blossom Public School, Manjeri - Excellence in Education Since 2003

Who We Are

Established in 2003, Blossom Public School in Manjeri has been a beacon of quality education in the region for over 20 years. As a Kerala State Board affiliated institution, we take pride in nurturing students with academic excellence, moral integrity, and cultural awareness.

Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing a supportive learning environment where students develop critical thinking skills, creativity, and ethical values that prepare them for future challenges.

At Blossom, we believe in a holistic approach to education that balances academic rigor with co-curricular activities, ensuring the all-round development of each child.

Our Vision

To nurture responsible global citizens who are academically proficient, morally upright, socially committed, and emotionally balanced, capable of meeting the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To provide quality education accessible to all sections of society, fostering academic excellence, moral values, physical fitness, and creative expression while respecting cultural diversity and promoting environmental consciousness.

Our Journey

2003

Founding Year

Blossom Public School was established with just 120 students and 8 teachers in a small building near Manjeri town.

2008

Campus Expansion

Moved to our current spacious campus with modern facilities including science labs, computer center, and sports grounds.

2013

10th Anniversary

Celebrated a decade of educational excellence with our first batch of students achieving 100% success in board examinations.

2018

Technology Integration

Implemented smart classrooms and digital learning resources across all grades to enhance the learning experience.

2023

20th Anniversary

Marked two decades of educational service with the inauguration of new arts and sports facilities and celebration of alumni achievements.
