Careers at Blossom Public School

At Blossom Public School, we believe that exceptional educators are the foundation of a transformative educational experience. We are constantly seeking passionate, innovative, and dedicated professionals who are committed to nurturing young minds and inspiring future leaders.

Working at Blossom means joining a collaborative community where your contributions are valued, your professional growth is supported, and your impact on students' lives is immeasurable. We offer competitive compensation, opportunities for advancement, and a positive work environment that fosters creativity and excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the selection process like?

Our selection process typically includes resume screening, an initial phone interview, a teaching demonstration, and a final panel interview with the school leadership team. We aim to make the process as thorough and transparent as possible.

Do you offer opportunities for professional development?

Yes, we invest heavily in our teachers' professional growth. We offer regular in-house training, workshops, conferences, and opportunities to pursue advanced certifications and degrees. We believe that when our teachers grow, our students benefit.

What benefits do you offer to faculty members?

We offer a competitive salary package, health insurance, retirement benefits, paid leave, childcare assistance for staff children, and subsidized accommodation for eligible employees. We also provide special incentives for outstanding performance.

How long does the application process take?

The entire process typically takes 3-4 weeks from application to final decision. However, this can vary depending on the position and the number of applications received. We strive to keep all candidates informed throughout the process.
